Lex Mundi Pro Bono Foundation Award.

Udo Udoma & Belo-Osagie is delighted to have been a recipient of the Lex Mundi Pro Bono Foundation Award together with other awardees, Clarke Gittens Farmer, Davis Graham & Stubbs LLP, Pestalozzi Attorneys at Law, and Romulo Law Offices, our fellow Lex Mundi member firms at the 14th Annual Lex Mundi Pro Bono Foundation Award which was held in San Francisco on Thursday, 4 May 2023. Our firm was lauded for our exceptional pro bono contributions and support in providing critical pro bono legal services and support to social impact organisation.

TrustLaw Collaboration Award.

Udo Udoma & Belo-Osagie is honoured to have been nominated for the TrustLaw Collaboration Award, one of the awards to be presented at the 2023 TrustLaw Awards organised by the Thomson Reuters Foundation. The Collaboration Award showcases highly effective working relationships between pro bono providers and recipients that exemplify the power and best practices of pro bono.

IFLR Africa Awards 2022.

UUBO has performed exceptionally in this year’s IFLR Africa Awards 2022, receiving a total of 5 awards across 3 jurisdictional and 2 deal categories, namely: Nigeria – Law Firm of the Year; Loans Firm of the Year; M&A Firm of the Year; Loans Deal of the Year; and M&A Deal of the Year.

The #IFLRAfricaAwards provide a unique opportunity to celebrate leading firms and individuals behind the most innovative and challenging legal work.
